Introduction

Solar energy is one of the most promising renewable energy sources of the future. In this project, we explore how solar energy harnesses the power of the sun through the principles of physics, while focusing on smart applications like solar panels, energy storage, and sustainable solutions. From understanding the fundamental processes to exploring the cutting-edge technologies, this project aims to provide insights into how solar energy can shape a greener, smarter future.
